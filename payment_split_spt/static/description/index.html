Primary Features

  • Create multiple split payments of invoices with easy interface.

  • Multiple payments for invoices at single click.

  • Choose different payment methods in single payment.

  • Supports Odoo Community as well as Enterprise versions.

In invoice screen, click on the "Register Payment" for make payment as default.




Select multiple journal and split total amount between all journal.




You can choose to keep open different amount or make invoice done with different as write-off.




Just one click and all done.
